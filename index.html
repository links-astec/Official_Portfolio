Research.

📄 Published — IJSET 2023
Design of a TinyML-Based Predictive Heart Disease Screening Device
Acquah Andrews, Quaye Nii Attoh Gabriel · KNUST, Computer Engineering · 2023, Vol. 11 Issue 6
Cardiovascular disorders are a leading global health concern. This study deploys a Shallow Neural Network (SNN) model on an Arduino BLE 33 Sense for heart failure prediction using TinyML, achieving 82.61% accuracy and 92.15% ROC-AUC. The research explores TinyML's potential for predictive healthcare on resource-constrained devices.
